Founding and Early Success

Oswal Soap Group was established by Late Shri Uttamchand Deshraj Jain in 1956 in Johari Bazaar, Jaipur. Driven by his hard work and dedication, the soap quickly gained popularity due to its high quality, making it a preferred choice among consumers. The foundation laid by Late Shri Jain was crucial in setting the stage for future growth.

Expansion and Growth

Following the initial success, Late Shri Uttamchand Deshraj Jain’s sons took significant steps to formalize and expand the business. They registered the company as “Oswal Soap Group” and began handling the operations more systematically. This expansion included setting up production centers and stores to cater to various cities, towns, and villages.

Product Diversification

In 1995, the third generation of the Deshraj family took over the business, introducing a broader range of products to meet diverse needs. The Oswal Soap Group expanded its offerings to include washing powder, detergent powder, bath soap, cleaning powders, and other daily-use items such as tea, spices, and sanitary products. This diversification aimed to cater to a wider market and enhance consumer satisfaction.

Nationwide Presence and Future Plans

With its products available at reasonable prices across Rajasthan, Punjab, Haryana, Gujarat, Maharashtra, and Madhya Pradesh, Oswal Soap Group has built a strong presence. The company continues to innovate and plans to launch new products in the near future. Ongoing research and efforts are dedicated to expanding their product line and reaching new markets.

Industry Leadership and Opportunities

With over 65 years of experience and a billion satisfied customers, Oswal Soap Group has become one of the largest manufacturers of daily-use commodities. The company operates through a network of over 1000 distributors, 250,000 wholesale retailers, and 800+ employees.
